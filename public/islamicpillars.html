The Five Pillars of Islam

Core beliefs and practices in Islam.

Shahada (Faith)

Declaring belief in the oneness of Allah and Muhammad as His messenger.

Salah (Prayer)

Performing five daily prayers at prescribed times.

Zakat (Charity)

Giving a portion of wealth (usually 2.5%) to those in need.

Sawm (Fasting)

Fasting from dawn to sunset during Ramadan.

Hajj (Pilgrimage)

Performing the pilgrimage to Mecca at least once if able.
